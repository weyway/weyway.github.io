Producer's Live Letter 87

W'eylyn Mavourneen June 21, 2025

Patch 7.25 - Community Feedback

Occult Crescent & Cosmic Exploration

This Live Letter starts with a Q&A about some issues/queries that were gathered by the community and developer teams.

Yoshi touches the topic of the team not improving processes to deal with their increased scale, which leads to declined quality of service and more bugs to hotfix.

Occult Crescent Feedback

Q:I wish I could enter the Forked Tower with a preformed alliance.

A:Yoshi felt the support systems in DRS were too high, they wanted to reduce these.

As of right now, it's hard to get into the same instance with 48 people, leading to what people callÂ  "island gacha" trying to go into the instance with the full premade.

They will implement a feature in patch 7.3 to allow a premade alliance to queue in to the same instance at once.

It was also mentioned that a list view to see who submitted cyphers and how many were submitted by each person will be coming.

Â 

Q: I wish there was a normal and savage version of the Forked Tower, like there was with Delubrum Reginae.

A: They refer to the lack of normal and savage modes for Forked Tower as a cost limitation (budget or time?)

For this first zone it will have to be like this, but for future zones in the Occult Crescent, they will try to have both a normal and a savage version of the instances.

Â 

Q: When magic pot and happy bunny coffers contain only materia and fireworks, the rewards feel inadequate for the effort required.

A: While the coffers also give gil as a reward, they understand that they were off on how rewarding these feel.

They will be discussing this further and trying to change it for the better.

Â 

Q: Enemies in FATEs can be defeated too quickly. Unless you're paying close attention, a FATE may be completed before you've even arrived.

A: This is not something that is limited to the Occult Crescent instance, but it's difficult to adjust parameters to match when an instance is crowded and when it's empty, on the fly.

They feel as a general rule it's better to have these parameters adjusted to lean on the side of easier rather than harder but they will be looking into the parameters and try to finetune them.Â 

Â 

Q: Please increase the drop rate for demiatma and soul shards.

A: This is down to personal RNG (luck) and they will not be buffing the drop rates for now.

For transparency, they provided the current drop rates:

  • Critical Encounter drop rate is at: 20%
  • FATE drop rate is at: 5%

It's also mentioned that back in the grind for the ARR relic weapon, atma drop rates at content release had only a 2% drop rate, that was steadily increased over time and now sits at 20%.

They want to keep random drops as a feature in the game and not have players just collect everything at once and abandon the content.

Â 

Q: The window for auroral mirages is too short, making it impossible to get there in time if you were in critical encounter.

A: They're changing the system around theÂ Auroral Mirage weather pattern.

They will make it so that the weather condition will not spawn until the instance environment allows for it, meaning that there's no active Critical Encounters at the time.

This will push the weather condition back as it aligns with instance content but otherwise won't change the amount of it spawning.

Chat Bubbles

They're basically done with the feature but it's still under active development.

They're still working out the implementation, my guess is basically settings and customization implementations (as seen in the image below there will be different coloured bubbles for players, npcs, etc, which might have new settings added to the game) as well as things like range and all of those considerations that come with this.

  • Will be optional and disabled by default
  • Text will continue to appear in the chat log as normal
  • Can be turned ON/OFF according to chat mode (say, shout, fc, etc)
  • Bubble and Font colour can be set according to chat mode too
  • Can be disabled during battle and PvP
  • Can be set to display 1 or 2 rows of text
  • Text will be truncated after a certain limit (check chat logs for that)
